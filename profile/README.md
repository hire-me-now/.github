# hire-me.now

Small, focused tools for the job hunt — ones that respect your time and keep your data yours.

### What I'm building

- **[Resume Tailor](https://resume.hire-me.now)** — keep one base resume as structured data.
  For every posting, AI adapts it to the role and ATS, drafts a matching cover letter,
  finds recruiters to reach out to, and the renderer turns it all into clean, consistent PDFs.

More tools to come.

### Bring your own key, keep your data

You use your own Claude or OpenAI key — it lives in your browser and talks straight to the
provider, **never our servers**. Your resume only ever leaves your browser to be rendered
into a PDF. No accounts, no tracking of your content.
